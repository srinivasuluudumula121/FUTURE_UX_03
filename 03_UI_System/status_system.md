# Status System

ClientFlow CRM uses a clear status system to improve visibility and help agencies prioritize work.

## Lead Status

* New Lead
* Contacted
* Meeting Scheduled
* Proposal Sent
* Negotiation
* Converted

---

## Client Health Status

### Healthy

Active and progressing normally.

### At Risk

Requires attention or delayed response.

### Waiting Feedback

Pending client response.

### Pending Payment

Invoice or payment pending.

### Completed

Successfully delivered project.

---

## Task Status

### Upcoming

Task scheduled.

### In Progress

Work currently active.

### Overdue

Missed deadline or delayed task.

### Completed

Finished successfully.

The status system helps users quickly scan operational priorities and reduce missed follow-ups.
