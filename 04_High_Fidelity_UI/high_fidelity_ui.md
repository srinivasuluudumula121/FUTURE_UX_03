# High-Fidelity UI Design

The final UI for ClientFlow CRM was designed to provide a scalable and workflow-focused experience for small agencies managing leads, clients, and follow-ups.

The interface follows modern B2B SaaS product principles with a strong focus on clarity, scalability, and operational visibility.

## Design Goals

The dashboard was designed to feel:

* professional
* scalable
* modern
* organized
* easy to scan
* operationally efficient

The visual hierarchy prioritizes action-first decision making for agency teams.

---

## Key Screens Designed

### Dashboard

The dashboard provides a centralized overview of agency operations.

Key areas include:

* KPI cards
* upcoming follow-ups
* overdue tasks
* client activity
* quick visibility into business health

---

### Lead Pipeline

The lead pipeline uses a visual kanban system to track lead movement across stages.

Stages include:

* New Lead
* Contacted
* Meeting Scheduled
* Proposal Sent
* Negotiation
* Converted

This improves visibility and sales tracking.

---

### Client Management

The client management screen helps agencies organize active clients.

The screen supports:

* client search
* filtering
* project visibility
* ownership tracking
* engagement visibility

---

### Client Profile

The client profile page provides detailed client visibility.

The page includes:

* client details
* communication history
* project information
* engagement tracking
* notes
* follow-ups

---

### Tasks & Follow-Ups

The task system helps reduce missed work.

The screen supports:

* reminders
* due dates
* overdue visibility
* completion tracking

---

### Analytics

The analytics dashboard provides operational insights.

It supports:

* lead conversion tracking
* performance visibility
* engagement monitoring
* business insights

---

### Settings

The settings page allows CRM customization and workflow preferences.

---

## UX Focus

The dashboard intentionally prioritizes:

* workflow clarity
* reduced operational confusion
* fast scanning
* scalable data visibility
* task prioritization
* better agency productivity

---

## Product Thinking

The CRM was designed around realistic agency workflows rather than isolated UI screens.

The experience supports:

Lead → Client → Follow-Up → Task → Delivery → Retention

This improves workflow continuity and operational efficiency.
