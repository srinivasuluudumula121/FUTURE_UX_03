# Competitive Research

The following CRM products were studied to understand modern SaaS dashboard UX patterns.

## HubSpot CRM

Studied for:

* dashboard hierarchy
* lead management
* sales visibility

## Pipedrive CRM

Studied for:

* lead pipeline design
* workflow clarity
* conversion tracking

## Zoho CRM

Studied for:

* scalable data management
* client organization
* enterprise workflows

## Key Learnings

Modern CRM products prioritize:

* action-first dashboards
* fast information scanning
* scalable layouts
* reusable components
* clear workflow visibility
* follow-up prioritization

These learnings informed the structure and UX decisions of ClientFlow CRM.
