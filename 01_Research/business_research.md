# Business Research

## Project Name

ClientFlow CRM

## Product Type

B2B SaaS CRM Dashboard

## Industry

Web Design / Digital Agency Management

## Business Context

ClientFlow CRM was designed for a small web design and development agency with approximately 5 team members managing 30–50 active clients and leads.

Many small agencies still rely on:

* spreadsheets
* WhatsApp communication
* scattered notes
* disconnected productivity tools

This creates operational inefficiencies and poor visibility.

## Current Problems

Agencies often struggle with:

* missed follow-ups
* poor lead tracking
* unclear client visibility
* scattered communication
* delayed task management
* operational confusion

As agencies grow, manual workflows become increasingly difficult to manage.

## Product Goal

The goal of ClientFlow CRM is to centralize agency workflows into one dashboard that helps teams:

* manage leads
* track active clients
* organize follow-ups
* monitor engagement
* improve operational visibility
* reduce missed opportunities
