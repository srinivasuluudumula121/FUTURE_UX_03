# Workflow Logic

ClientFlow CRM was designed around a realistic agency workflow.

## Lead Management Workflow

Potential clients enter the CRM as leads.

Leads move through stages:

New Lead
→ Contacted
→ Meeting Scheduled
→ Proposal Sent
→ Negotiation
→ Converted

Converted leads automatically become active clients.

---

## Client Management Workflow

Active clients are managed through:

* project tracking
* engagement monitoring
* communication history
* task visibility
* status updates

---

## Follow-Up Workflow

Follow-ups are converted into actionable tasks.

This helps agencies avoid:

* missed communication
* forgotten reminders
* delayed responses

---

## Task Management Workflow

Tasks are assigned, tracked, and completed through a centralized system to improve accountability and execution.
