# Wireframing Process

Low-fidelity wireframes were created to structure workflows before visual design.

The wireframes focused on:

* dashboard hierarchy
* workflow visibility
* scalable layout structure
* fast information scanning
* action prioritization

## Screens Explored

### Dashboard

Operational overview and priority actions.

### Lead Pipeline

Visual lead movement across stages.

### Client Management

Client list and engagement visibility.

### Client Profile

Detailed client overview and communication tracking.

### Tasks & Follow-Ups

Task organization and reminder visibility.

### Analytics

Business insights and performance tracking.

## Design Focus

The wireframes intentionally prioritized:

* workflow clarity
* operational efficiency
* reduced friction
* scalability
* B2B SaaS usability
