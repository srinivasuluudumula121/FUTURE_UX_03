## Prototype Goal

The prototype was created to simulate a realistic CRM workflow for a small agency managing leads, clients, tasks, and follow-ups.

The experience focuses on operational clarity, workflow continuity, and daily usability.

---

## Prototype Experience

Users can explore:

### Dashboard
- KPI overview
- urgent actions
- recent activity
- quick operational visibility

### Lead Pipeline
- lead movement across stages
- kanban workflow
- conversion tracking

### Client Management
- client list
- filtering
- search
- engagement visibility

### Client Profile
- client details
- notes
- communication history
- follow-up tracking

### Tasks & Follow-Ups
- reminders
- overdue tasks
- task completion

### Analytics
- performance insights
- lead conversion visibility
- agency health metrics

---

## UX Goal

The prototype was designed to evaluate:

- workflow clarity
- navigation usability
- task visibility
- operational efficiency
- scalable CRM interactions

The interaction flow was intentionally designed to feel like a modern B2B SaaS product used by agencies daily.
