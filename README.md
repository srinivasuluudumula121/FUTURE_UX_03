# ClientFlow CRM – B2B SaaS CRM Dashboard

UI/UX Design Project for Future Interns – Task 03

---

## Project Overview

ClientFlow CRM is a B2B SaaS CRM dashboard designed for a **5-member web design and development agency managing 30–50 clients and leads**.

The goal of this project was to centralize agency workflows into one platform and improve lead tracking, client management, follow-ups, and operational visibility.

The CRM was designed to solve workflow confusion caused by spreadsheets, messaging tools, and disconnected systems.

The experience focuses on:

- workflow clarity
- operational efficiency
- lead tracking
- task organization
- scalable business management
- B2B SaaS usability

---

## Problem Statement

Many small agencies still manage operations using:

- spreadsheets
- WhatsApp communication
- scattered notes
- disconnected productivity tools

This creates several operational problems:

- missed follow-ups
- poor lead visibility
- delayed communication
- task confusion
- unclear ownership
- operational inefficiency

As agencies grow, these manual workflows become difficult to manage.

ClientFlow CRM solves this by centralizing agency operations into one scalable dashboard.
